About Our Team

1803 Vikings Robotics is a student-led FIRST Robotics Competition team from Paul D. Schreiber High School in Port Washington, NY. Founded in 2006, we bring together passionate students in not only CAD, programming, and circuitry, but also students in art, marketing, and business. We produce future engineers and leaders through hands-on STEM experiences, community outreach, and collaboration.

Meet the Vikings

Our members are organized into subteams: Mechanical, Programming, Electrical, CAD, Strategy, and Media. Together, we build more than robots—we build leadership, creativity, and teamwork.
